### 常见问题

#### 1. 价格为什么和银行、金店或其他 App 不一样？

本应用展示的是**第三方公开参考数据**，不是柜台成交价，也不是 tick 级实时行情。

- **LBMA 模式**：Minted Metal 提供的伦敦 PM 定盘价**参考**（第三方来源，**非 LBMA 官方**）；工作日约更新 2 次；App 内最长缓存 15 分钟  
- **长期参考**：世界银行 Pink Sheet **月度**名义美元金价（$/盎司）；最新月份可能滞后发布；**非 LBMA 定盘价**

不同数据来源、不同更新频率之间的价差属于正常现象。本应用**不提供**黄金买卖、报价或任何金融交易服务。

#### 2. 数据多久更新？需要手动刷新吗？

- 打开 App 或**下拉刷新**时会尝试拉取新数据  
- 默认**不自动刷新**；可在「设置」中开启 30 分钟或 1 小时自动刷新  
- LBMA 数据最长缓存 15 分钟；长期参考与汇率最长缓存 24 小时  

#### 3. 显示加载失败或一直无法刷新怎么办？

请依次尝试：

1. 检查网络连接（Wi‑Fi 或蜂窝数据）  
2. 下拉刷新  
3. 完全关闭 App 后重新打开  
4. 若仍失败，可能是第三方数据源暂时不可用，请稍后再试  

本应用依赖 Minted Metal、World Bank、Frankfurter 等公开服务。第三方 API 中断时，部分功能可能暂时不可用。汇率拉取失败时会尝试使用设备上的**本地缓存**。

#### 4. 「长期参考」、走势图和「日期对比」是什么？

- **长期参考**使用世界银行**月度**数据；界面显示「数据月份」，而非具体某一天  
- 走势图按**当前汇率**换算历史美元价格，长周期可能存在小幅误差  
- **日期对比**：选择较早的月份，与最新月份价格对比涨跌幅度

- 所有价格与换算结果仅供参考，请勿作为唯一决策依据

### FAQ

#### 1. Why doesn’t the price match my bank, jeweler, or another app?

The App shows **third-party public reference data**, not counter prices or tick-level live quotes.

- **LBMA mode**: London PM fix **references** via Minted Metal (third-party, **not official LBMA**); ~2 updates on business days; up to 15 min cache in the App  
- **Long-term**: World Bank Pink Sheet **monthly** nominal USD/toz; latest month may lag; **not an LBMA fix**

Differences between sources and update schedules are normal. The App provides **no** trading, quoting, or brokerage services.

#### 2. How often is data updated? Do I need to refresh manually?

- Data is fetched when you **open the App** or **pull to refresh**  
- **Auto-refresh is off by default**; optional 30 min / 1 h in Settings  
- LBMA cache: up to 15 min; long-term & FX cache: up to 24 h  

#### 3. Loading failed or refresh keeps spinning?

Try in order:

1. Check your network (Wi‑Fi or cellular)  
2. Pull to refresh  
3. Force-quit and reopen the App  
4. If it still fails, a third-party source may be temporarily down—try again later  

The App relies on Minted Metal, World Bank, and Frankfurter. If FX fetch fails, a **local cache** may be used.

#### 4. What are long-term mode, charts, and date comparison?

- **Long-term** uses World Bank **monthly** data; the UI shows a **data month**, not a specific day  
- Charts convert historical USD prices using **current FX**; long ranges may have small errors  
- **Date comparison**: pick an earlier month and compare vs the latest month  

#### 5. Is this investment advice or official LBMA data?

**No.** The App is a reference and conversion tool only:

- **Not** investment, trading, or financial advice  
- **Not** affiliated with or authorized by LBMA, any exchange, or bank  
- All prices and conversions are for reference only—do not rely on the App as your sole basis for decisions  

See the full [Disclaimer](Disclaimer.md).

#### 6. Does the App collect personal data?

**No.** No sign-in required; no ads or tracking. Preferences and price cache stay **on your device only**. See [Privacy Policy](Privacy%20Policy.md).

#### 7. How do I change language, currency, dark mode, or data source?

Tap **Settings** (gear icon, top-right):

- Language, appearance (dark / light / system)  
- Currency, weight unit (gram / troy oz / kg)  
- Auto-refresh interval  
- LBMA / long-term data source  
- Data source notes, Disclaimer & Privacy Policy links
